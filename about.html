ABOUT COMPANY

With our specially prepared Machine learning algorithms on the acquired database

*In which areas the development of plants is less than the norm or is underdeveloped
* In which areas it is normal
*Anomalies of the soil surface
*Recruitment of areas already circled (in areas of land).

* You will no longer need dozens of manpower, energy loss, time loss, additional equipment and large financial costs.
